Modrinth App

Official Modrinth launcher — clean, fast and mod-friendly.

1
Download all three .jar files
Get Fabric API, OpSec Mod (both on Modrinth) and Novoware. All must be Minecraft 1.21.11 version.
2
Create a Fabric 1.21.11 profile
In Modrinth App click + Create Profile → set version to 1.21.11 → loader to Fabric → confirm.
3
Open the mods folder
Click ··· next to your profile → Open Folder → open the mods/ subfolder inside.
4
Drop all three .jars in
Drag fabric-api.jar, opsec.jar and novoware.jar into the mods folder.
5
Launch and press Right Shift
Hit Play. Once in-game press Right Shift to open the Novoware GUI.

Why does this page load slowly?

To keep everything in a single file, the Novoware .jar is embedded directly inside this webpage using Base64 encoding — a method that converts binary files into plain text so a browser can store them inside HTML. Base64 inflates the file size by about 33%, which takes the original 9.3MB jar up to 12.4MB of encoded text inside the page. That's why the site may feel slower to load than a normal webpage — your browser is downloading the entire client in the background before the page finishes. When you click any download button, the browser instantly decodes it back to the exact original file and saves it to your computer. The downloaded jar will always be the correct 9.3MB — nothing added, nothing changed.
